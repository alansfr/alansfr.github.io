Our Guides
Our company begins and ends with our Guides. Nothing is more important to us than finding the right English-speaking, friendly male "Cariocas"
(residents of Rio) to guide you on your trip and to help you create exactly the experience you want to have.

Whether you want a charming young companion for the week or a vibrant, friendly guy to spend a day showing you the ins and outs of Rio, we can provide you with the perfect guide compatible with your desires and goals for your trip.

Our screening process for selecting our guides is extremely rigorous. Most of the guides we offer are young men whom we know personally in some
capacity from our living in Rio. They are among the most reliable and trustworthy
people we know. They know all aspects of the city - from its steamy saunas and clubs at night to its stunning beaches - and are ready to accompany you on any adventure safely, non-judgmentally, and with a devotion to showing you a great time in their city.

In short, we ensure that our guides are energetic, charming, knowledgeable, responsible and handsome young men who know how to show you a great time in Rio. They are able to help you create your ideal Rio experience no matter what
it is you want to see and do.
